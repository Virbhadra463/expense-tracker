UPI QR Scanner

Scan. Log.
Pay. Done.

Open the app before any UPI payment. Scan the merchant QR, fill in the amount, and confirm — the expense is pre-logged before you even pay.

1
Scan the Merchant QR

Use the built-in in-browser QR scanner. No separate app needed.

2
Fill Expense Details

Date is auto-filled. Add amount, category and note, then confirm.

3
Pay via Your UPI App

You're redirected to GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm to complete payment.

4
Confirm Payment Status

Mark as "Payment Done" or "Not Done". Failed transactions are never counted.
